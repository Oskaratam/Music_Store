A True Work of Art

The PRS SE Silver Sky is a familiar iteration of the original Silver Sky model that was first introduced with John Mayer in 2018. This workhorse guitar provides ample tone and playability in four distinct colors, delivering looks, feel, and sound.

The PRS SE Silver Sky starts with a poplar body, bolt-on maple neck, and rosewood fretboard with PRS trademark bird inlays. The 22-fret, 25.5” scale length neck features the original 635JM carve and an 8.5” fretboard radius, so the PRS SE Silver Sky will feel right at home. Plug it in, and the three single-coil 635JM “S” pickups will bark and sing. This guitar is anchored by a two-point steel tremolo, synthetic bone nut, and vintage-style tuners. Other design features include the PRS Silver Sky inverted headstock shape and the PRS double-acting truss rod (accessible from the front of the headstock for ease of use). Ships with PRS Classic 10-46 strings and gig bag.

PRS guitars are perfect for both beginners and experienced guitarists. These are high-quality instruments that provide comfort when playing and rich classical sound.

About Muse Vibe

Who we are?

Music Vibe was born from the dreams of aspiring musicians who understood the importance of quality instruments and exceptional service. Founded by a group of individuals deeply passionate about music, we set out on a journey to create a music store that truly resonated with the needs and desires of fellow musicians.

Quality

We understand that the heart and soul of any musical performance lies in the instruments themselves. That's why, at Music Vibe, we go the extra mile to ensure that every instrument we offer is of the highest quality. Before reaching your hands, each instrument is carefully inspected, tuned, and set up by our team of experienced masters. This meticulous attention to detail guarantees that you receive an instrument that not only meets but exceeds your expectations.

Community

At Music Vibe, we don't just sell instruments; we cultivate a vibrant community of musicians who share a passion for music. Join our growing community, and you'll find inspiration, resources, and support from fellow musicians and our dedicated team.

Store

Our physical store, nestled in the heart of Kyiv, is more than just a place to buy instruments – it's a gathering place for artists to connect, collaborate, and jam together.

Step inside our store, and you'll immediately feel the warm, inviting atmosphere. Our comfortable seating areas invite musicians to linger, chat, and exchange creative ideas. We often host open jam sessions, where local talents come together to explore new sounds and styles, creating an environment where musical magic happens spontaneously.
